About Us

AmaliTech recruits graduates into the AmaliTech Training Academies to undergo its free training programme in IT/Digital skills offering diverse employment pathway opportunities.

Our Program

We are offering a blended learning approach in a mix of online and on-site learning. The courses provided are all self-paced and instructor supported. Our training is mainly focused on software development and engineering. We will be offering specialisations in front-end development, back-end development, full stack development and Java programming.

Front-end

Starting with the skills to develop, functionalize, and make an interactive web app with languages like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, etc

Back End

By the end of this specialisation, you will be able to work on server and client-side interfaces and create databases according to the application architecture.

full Stack

This specialisation offers front-end and back-end development expertise, integrating both sides and developing fully functional web applications..
